Data Deletion Instructions

We value your privacy and provide the option to delete your personal data. Follow the instructions below to request data deletion.

Steps to Delete Your Data

  1. Log in to your account on our website.
  2. Navigate to the Account Settings section.
  3. Find the Privacy or Data Management section.
  4. Click on the Delete My Data option and follow the prompts.

Contact Support

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at support@yourwebsite.com. Provide your account information and a brief description of your request.

For Facebook Users

If you signed up using Facebook, you can also remove our app and request data deletion directly from your Facebook account:

  1. Go to your Facebook account settings.
  2. Navigate to Apps and Websites.
  3. Find and select our app.
  4. Click on Remove to disconnect the app and initiate data deletion.

Additional Information

For more details on how we handle data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
